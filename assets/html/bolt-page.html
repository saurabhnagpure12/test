QUICKER, SMARTER, SAFER
Ermin intends to construct a sophisticated Bot capable of transporting objects within an 8-km radius. Objective is to create a delivery platform which introduces a new way for Instant Deliveries that can be used any time at substantially less cost.
WHY ERMIN BOLT?
Eco-Friendly
Being an Electric Bot, the product will be eco-friendly and carbon negative as well. Hence, being both energy, cost efficient and safe for the environment and customers.
Efficient
The Bot can be used to deliver packages, groceries, and food straight from stores in the community, by making a request using a mobile application. After placing an order, the entire route of the bot can be tracked.
Vision
Aim to make last mile delivery Quicker, Smarter, Safer, and more Cost Effective.
