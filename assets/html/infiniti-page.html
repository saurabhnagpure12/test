INFINITY & BEYOND
ERMIN INFINI-T is a pay and ride electric commuter bicycle which comes with a range of upto 25kms. It is a smart lock enabled bicycle which requires the user to unlock the cycle through our INFINI-T mobile application. The cycles will be placed at a docking station which houses a charger, locking system and many more. This allows for easy commute in and around Public Areas, Communities, Universities and Corporate Offices which enhances shared mobility ecosystems.
WHY ERMIN INFINI-T?
Eco-Friendly
Utilising a battery for propulsion, the INFINI-T reduces the need for fuel based transportation. In turn reducing release of harmful gases.
Efficient
Using top of the line brushless motors provides a smooth and hitchless performance.
Economic
With a 7.5Ah battery, the ERMIN INFINI-T only takes Rs. 6 worth of electricity to completely charge.
Effortless
With a top speed of 25kmph and a lightweight frame the INFINI-T makes short distance commute seems like a piece of cake.
